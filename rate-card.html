Branding & Rebranding

Branding is the process of creating a clear and consistent identity for a business, organisation, product, or service. It helps people recognise, remember, and trust the business across different touchpoints such as logo, colours, typography, business cards, social media, brochures, websites, packaging, signboards, presentations, uniforms, and marketing materials. For any business in any sector, proper branding creates a professional first impression, improves market positioning, builds customer confidence, and gives the business a consistent visual and communication system that can be used in daily operations, marketing, sales, partnerships, and expansion.

Services include:
Logo design, brand identity, rebranding support, brand kit, stationery, and basic brand guideline.

Starting from ₹7,500

Branding Structure

Branding & Rebranding

06

Branding is not limited to logo design. A logo is only one part of a business identity. A proper brand identity works like a visual SOP for the business, giving clear rules on how the brand should look and be used across documents, social media, websites, presentations, signboards, uniforms, invoices, quotations, brochures, packaging, and customer-facing communication. It helps business owners, employees, designers, agencies, printers, and marketing teams maintain consistency whenever anything is created for the brand. Velnex's Branding & Rebranding service supports new and existing businesses by creating or improving their logo, colour system, typography, brand usage rules, stationery, digital profile assets, document templates, and basic brand guideline structure.
